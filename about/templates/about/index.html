{% trans "Our Philosophy" %}

{% trans "Maintain transparency and everything else will follow" %}

{% blocktrans %} Our ultimate purpose is to bring positive impacts to our clients, our stakeholders, and our partners. The profit we generate is derived from investing in startups and from offering counseling services to our clients. To ensure that our purpose to be achieved, we need to evaluate every company thoroughly. Evaluation is never an easy journey, and promising startups do not always have a good ending, but a lack of transparency even makes it worse. It distracts our eyes and keeps us from unleashing the true potential of our clients. Therefore, We require our clients to be as transparent as possible to make their dream true and make the impossible possible. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Great companies start with great leaders." %}

{% blocktrans %} Founder-led companies outperform the rest. The most world's largest companies have been founded and run by founders. And the influence of the founders is surprisingly powerful and lasts for a long time. Depends on the founders' entrepreneurship and leadership, any failing company can succeed, and any scalable company can fail. We, therefore, have high expectations of founders in terms of investment. We expect founders to have a vision for the future, enthusiasm for creating value, and strong fundamentals of business. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Environmental sustainablity is part of our mission." %}

{% blocktrans %} Climate change is one of the greatest threats of our time, putting everyone's life at risk. We are well aware of the urgency of climate change and the need for decisive actions. To save our planet, we remain committed to our mission: scale up investments in sustainable startups. Every investment we make will have a critical measurement of energy efficiency. This does not necessarily limit our investment range or opportunities. We have a firm belief that well-designed products have a lower environmental footprint as well. We also hope our continual effort with environmental conservation motivates others to join us. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Success always takes help." %}

{% blocktrans %} Before we begin, we would like to quote Melinda Gates: "If you are successful, it is because somewhere, sometime, someone gave you a life or an idea that started you in the right direction." We do investment, but seed money does not come out of nowhere. We also get invested by limited partners who are willing to join our wills. Our job is to make the flow of money, scale up businesses, and innovate the world. Therefore, to accelerate the flow of money, we expect our partners to do the same thing: take social responsibility and benefit society to make the world a better place. {% endblocktrans %}
